PER BET TYPE — CONTENT BRIEFS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BET TYPE 1: MATCH RESULT (1X2)
- Most common market in football
- Three outcomes per match
- BetsPlug uses Elo difference + Poisson model
- Predictable when Elo gap large
- Less predictable in derby/cup matches

BET TYPE 2: DOUBLE CHANCE
- Cover two outcomes (1X, X2, 12)
- Lower variance, lower odds
- BetsPlug favors Double Chance for medium-confidence picks
- Good for reducing risk on uncertain matches

BET TYPE 3: BOTH TEAMS TO SCORE (BTTS)
- Will both teams score? Yes/No
- BetsPlug uses Poisson goal models
- Highly predictable in high-scoring leagues (Bundesliga, Eredivisie)
- Less predictable in defensive leagues (Serie A historically)

BET TYPE 4: OVER/UNDER GOALS
- Total goals over/under threshold (1.5, 2.5, 3.5 most common)
- BetsPlug specializes in 2.5 line predictions
- Highly statistical market — Poisson distribution direct application
- Strong predictor when expected goal totals diverge from line

BET TYPE 5: ASIAN HANDICAP
- Eliminates draw outcome via handicap
- Common in Asian markets, growing globally
- BetsPlug covers but with smaller sample for accuracy
- Better suited for matches with clear favorite

BET TYPE 6: CORRECT SCORE
- Predict exact final score
- Highest variance, highest odds
- BetsPlug shows probability matrix from Poisson model
- Most likely score per match identified, not "predicted"

BET TYPE 7: HALF-TIME / FULL-TIME
- Predict result at half-time AND full-time
- 9 possible outcomes per match
- BetsPlug uses split Poisson model (HT and FT separately)
- Niche market, smaller sample for AI training

BET TYPE 8: DRAW NO BET
- Eliminates draw from 1X2
- Stake returned if draw
- Lower variance than 1X2
- BetsPlug uses similar inputs as 1X2 with adjustment

QUESTIONS (6)

Q1: "Which bet type is most predictable?"
A: "Statistically, Over/Under 2.5 goals and BTTS markets are most 
   predictable using Poisson models. They have well-defined 
   probability distributions and large historical sample sizes."

Q2: "Why does BetsPlug recommend specific bet types per match?"
A: "Our engine identifies the highest-confidence prediction for 
   each match — the market where statistical signal is strongest. 
   This varies per match: sometimes 1X2 has clearest signal, 
   sometimes Over/Under, sometimes BTTS."

Q3: "Can I see all bet type predictions for a single match?"
A: "Paid subscribers see all market predictions per match. Free 
   users see the engine's highest-confidence pick per match."

Q4: "Why don't you predict specific player goalscorers?"
A: "Player-level predictions have much higher variance than 
   team-level predictions. Lineup uncertainty, individual form 
   variance, and tactical changes make accurate goalscorer 
   prediction unreliable. We focus on team-level markets where 
   accuracy is achievable."

Q5: "What's the difference between probability and confidence?"
A: "Probability is the mathematical likelihood we calculate 
   (e.g., 78% chance of Over 2.5). Confidence is our certainty 
   in that probability — affected by data quality, sample size, 
   and matching match characteristics. We display probability 
   numbers; high-confidence predictions are flagged separately."

Q6: "Why are some bet types only available on paid plans?"
A: "Free tier shows our highest-confidence pick per match — 
   typically 1X2. Other markets (Over/Under, BTTS, Asian Handicap) 
   require subscriber access. This balances free value with 
   sustainable subscription model."
