SECTIE 3: PRINCIPLES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

H2: "Responsible Gambling Principles"

Body:
"If you choose to gamble using our predictions or otherwise, 
consider these principles:

1. Set limits: Decide how much money you can afford to lose 
   before starting. Stick to that limit.

2. Time limits: Set time boundaries for gambling activities. 
   Don't let gambling consume your day.

3. Don't chase losses: Losing is part of gambling. Trying to 
   win back losses by betting more is the fastest path to bigger 
   problems.

4. Never gamble with money you need: Rent, food, bills — these 
   come first. Gambling money is entertainment money you can 
   afford to lose.

5. Don't gamble while emotional: Avoid betting when stressed, 
   angry, or drunk. Decisions made in these states are usually 
   poor.

6. Take breaks: Step away regularly. Long gambling sessions 
   reduce judgment.

7. Track your activity: Know how much you've actually spent over 
   time. Memory misleads.

8. Gambling isn't income: Treat it as entertainment that costs 
   money, not a way to make money."

SECTIE 4: SIGNS OF PROBLEM GAMBLING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

H2: "Recognizing Problem Gambling"

Body:
"Gambling becomes a problem when:

- You spend more than you intended or can afford
- You feel anxious or irritable when not gambling
- You lie about how much you gamble
- You chase losses with bigger bets
- You neglect work, family, or responsibilities
- You borrow money to gamble
- You feel guilt or shame about gambling
- You can't stop even when you want to

If you recognize these signs in yourself or someone close to you, 
help is available."
